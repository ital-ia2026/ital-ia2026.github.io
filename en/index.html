Welcome to Ital-IA 2026!

Ital-IA is the CINI National Conference on Artificial Intelligence, aimed at building common goals among public institutions, Italian industry, and the scientific research community. Ital-IA wants to be the national network connecting all initiatives in Italy to seize AI’s opportunities.

The conference is organized by the National Laboratory "Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems" (AIIS) of CINI, and by the Università Campus Bio-Medico di Roma.

Ital-IA and Summer School on Artificial Intelligence in Health and Life Science

The 2026 edition of Ital-IA runs in conjunction with the Summer School on Artificial Intelligence in Health and Life Science 2026, 15–19 June 2026. The Summer School is part of the National PhD in AI, offering intensive training on cutting-edge AI for medicine and life sciences, with international experts and a strong bridge to real-world applications.

Participation in the Summer School automatically includes access to Ital-IA 2026.
