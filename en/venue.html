Venue: Università Campus Bio-Medico di Roma

The conference will take place at Università Campus Bio-Medico di Roma, a university dedicated to integrating scientific research and healthcare. Located in Rome, it offers an inspiring setting for both academic activities and cultural exploration.

Address

Università Campus Bio-Medico di Roma
Via Álvaro del Portillo, 21
00128 Rome, Italy

How to reach UCBM

From Termini Station: Metro LINE B towards Laurentina to the terminus, then bus 72 for 13 stops (Alessandrini/Del Portillo) and 2 minutes on foot.

From Rome:
– Public transport: Metro B to Laurentina, then bus 72 for 13 stops (Alessandrini/Del Portillo) and a short walk.
– By car: GRA exit 25 – Laurentina towards Ardea – Università Campus Bio-Medico di Roma. Follow via di Vallerano then via Álvaro del Portillo to number 21.

From Fiumicino Airport: Train to Termini Station, then Metro B to Laurentina terminus and bus 72 for 13 stops.

From Ciampino Airport: Bus/shuttle to Termini Station, then Metro B to Laurentina terminus and bus 72 for 13 stops.
